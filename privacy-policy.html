Privacy Policy

How Ronny Edu Games collects, uses and protects information from visitors to this site.

Home › Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the website ronnygame.netlify.app ("Ronny Edu Games"), operated by Mwalimu Ronny. Please read it carefully before using the site.

1. Introduction

Ronny Edu Games is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit ronnygame.netlify.app, how that information is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

By using this website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Ronny Edu Games does not require you to create an account or log in to use any games on this site. No personal registration data is collected as a condition of access.

If you use the Contact page to send a message, you voluntarily provide your name, email address and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and is not stored in any database or shared with third parties.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this website, certain technical information may be automatically recorded by third-party services integrated into the site, including:

  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL (the page that directed you to this site)
  • Date and time of your visit

This information is collected and processed by third-party services described below, not by Ronny Edu Games directly.

2.3 Game Progress and Scores

All game scores, progress and session data on Ronny Edu Games are stored temporarily in your browser's memory (JavaScript variables) during your session only. This data is never sent to any server, never stored in cookies or localStorage, and is permanently lost when you close or refresh the page. No personal game data is ever transmitted or retained.

3. Cookies

Ronny Edu Games itself does not set cookies from its own domain. However, third-party services embedded in this site — specifically Google AdSense — may set cookies on your device in order to serve and personalise advertisements. These cookies are governed by Google's own privacy policy.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or selected cookies
  • Block all or selected cookies from being set
  • Block cookies from particular sites

Please note that blocking cookies may affect the functionality of certain third-party features on this site.

4. Google AdSense and Advertising

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is a service provided by Google LLC. As a third-party vendor, Google uses cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google collects and processes data in connection with AdSense, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

We comply with the Google Publisher Policies and Google Publisher Restrictions. We do not direct Google to serve personalised ads to visitors who we believe are under the age of 13.

5. Google Fonts

This site uses Google Fonts to load web fonts (Playfair Display and Source Sans 3). When you visit a page that uses Google Fonts, your browser sends a request to Google's servers, which may include your IP address. Google Fonts is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Netlify Hosting

This website is hosted on Netlify, Inc. Netlify may collect server access logs including IP addresses, browser information and pages accessed, as part of normal hosting operations. These logs are used for security, performance monitoring and abuse prevention. For more information, see Netlify's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Ronny Edu Games includes content suitable for children aged 8 and above. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. This site does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. No registration, login or personal data submission is required to use any game on this site.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to this site (for example, through the Contact form), please contact us immediately using the details on the Contact page and we will take steps to remove such information.

We do not direct personalised advertising to users we have reason to believe are under the age of 13, in compliance with applicable laws including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Publisher policies.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical precautions to protect information. This website is served over HTTPS, meaning all data transmitted between your browser and our server is encrypted in transit. As stated above, no personal data entered in games is ever transmitted to a server.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to external websites, including the creator's other educational sites (bestronny.netlify.app, cbcbest.co.ke). These sites have their own privacy policies, and Ronny Edu Games is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

Given that Ronny Edu Games collects very minimal personal data (only via the optional Contact form), exercising most of these rights is straightforward — simply contact us at the address below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, our practices, or third-party services. When we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the way your information is handled on this website, please contact us via the Contact page.
