Math Learning

Areas of Shapes

  • Square: Area = side × side
  • Rectangle: Area = length × width
  • Triangle: Area = (base × height)/2
  • Circle: Area = π × radius²

Volumes of Solids

  • Cube: Volume = side³
  • Cuboid: Volume = length × width × height
  • Cylinder: Volume = π × r² × h
  • Sphere: Volume = 4/3 × π × r³
